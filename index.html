Cunjian Chen is currently a Research Fellow at Monash Suzhou and an Adjunct Lecturer (Assistant Professor) at Monash University Australia. He obtained his Ph.D. in Computer Science from West Virginia University. Before joining Monash, he worked as a Senior Research Associate at Michigan State University. Dr. Chen's research interests focus on Generative Models and their applications in computer vision. He is also a Senior Member of IEEE and directs the Image and Vision Lab.

We have several openings with full scholarship support for PhD students interested in Monash University starting Fall 2023. The ideal applicant should be highly self-motivated and has strong skills in both mathematics and programming. If interested, please send me your CV. I am also looking for RA/Interns.
