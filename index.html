Cunjian Chen

 

Cunjian Chen, Senior Research Associate@MSU
iPRoBe Lab, Department of Computer Science and Engineering
Michigan State University, East Lansing, MI

Email / Google Scholar / Github / LinkedIn / Youtube

I received Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from West Virginia University in 2014. From August 2013 to August 2014, I was a Visiting Scholar in the Department of Computer Science Engineering at Michigan State University. Currently, I work as a Senior Research Associate at Michigan State University.

Research Interests

Face Detection; Face Alignment; Face Recognition; Soft Biometrics Estimation; Presentation Attack Detection.
