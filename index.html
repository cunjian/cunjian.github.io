Biography

I received Master and Ph.D. degrees in Computer Science from West Virginia University, in 2011 and 2014 respectively, supervised by Dr. Arun Ross. From August 2013 to August 2014, I am also a Visiting Scholar in the Department of Computer Science Engineering at Michigan State University. Please feel free to contact me at cunjian.chen AT outlook.com.
