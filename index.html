Cunjian Chen

 

Ph.D. West Virginia University
Postdoc Michigan State University

Email / Google Scholar / Github / Group

Cunjian Chen obtained his Ph.D. in Computer Science from West Virginia University under the supervision of Arun Ross and Antitza Dantcheva. Before joining Monash, he worked as a Senior Research Associate at Michigan State University with Arun Ross. His research interests focus on Generative Models and their applications in Computer Vision. He is also a Senior Member of IEEE and leads the Image and Vision Group.

Research Interests

  • Generative AI, Computer Vision, Deep Learning, Medical Image Analysis.
