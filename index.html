Cunjian Chen

iPRoBe Lab, Department of Computer Science and Engineering
Michigan State University, East Lansing, MI

Email / Google Scholar / Github / LinkedIn

I received M.S. and Ph.D. degrees in Computer Science from West Virginia University, in 2011 and 2014 respectively, supervised by Dr. Arun Ross. From August 2013 to August 2014, I was a Visiting Scholar in the Department of Computer Science Engineering at Michigan State University. Currently, I work as a Research Associate at Michigan State University with Dr. Arun Ross.

Research Interests

Face Detection; Face Alignment; Face Recognition; Soft Biometrics Estimation; Presentation Attack Detection.
