Cunjian Chen is currently a Research Fellow at Monash Suzhou and an Adjunct Lecturer (Assistant Professor) at Monash University Australia. He obtained his Ph.D. in Computer Science from West Virginia University. Before joining Monash, he worked as a Senior Research Associate with Arun Ross at Michigan State University. Dr. Chen's research interests focus on Generative Models and their applications in computer vision. He is also a Senior Member of IEEE and directs the Image and Vision Lab.

We are constantly seeking highly motivated students interested in pursuing a PhD at Monash University to join our research team. The ideal applicant should possess strong skills in both mathematics and programming. Preference will be given to applicants with publications in top venues. If you are interested, please send me your CV. Additionally, I am also looking for Research Assistants/Interns.
